    • LOOK AND FEEL OF THE SUBPAGES

    Why is the Look and Feel of the website important?

    It is important because it instantly conveys an attitude to clients

    Every website has a different purpose and the look and feel of it would help this client to achieve their purpose and who their audience are.

    It's like a "personality," which matches up with what the client wants

    Our Theme: A good look and feel of our website could be...

    • Approachable
    • Friendly
    • Experienced
    • w/ some Consistent parts (Too much consistency can create confusion to the audience because they don't notice when the page is changed.)

    Our Final Website LOOK AND FEEL is going to be..

    Color of background: White

    Logo: strong>The AIS Logo will help meet the client's objective..This Logo will make the audience (AIS HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS) recognize that this is for them to access and sign up for their school events. The students will be able to get who this site is for.

    Size: The size of the sign up sheet would be the biggest, since that is our purpose of making this site for our client.

    Tone: It would be friendly since it is for the AIS students who are willing to help people in the community
